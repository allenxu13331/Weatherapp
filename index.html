About Atmos

Built by Allen Xu as part of the PM Accelerator technical assessment.

PM Accelerator is a product management training and career accelerator program. It helps aspiring and current product managers build real-world skills through mentorship, hands-on projects, and a global community. The program prepares PMs to excel across industries by focusing on product strategy, execution, stakeholder management, and data-driven decision making.

Weather data: Open‑Meteo (geocoding, daily past + future up to 16 days) and optionally OpenWeather current conditions only on the Today tab for live temp, feels like, humidity, wind, pressure, and visibility.
